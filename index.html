Tetris

How to Play

Objective: Clear lines by filling rows with falling blocks (tetrominoes). Score points by clearing lines!

Controls:

  • Desktop: Arrow Keys or WASD (A/D for left/right, S for down, W for rotate clockwise, Z for counter-clockwise, Space for hard drop)
  • Mobile: Tap left/right sides of the game board to move, tap center to rotate, swipe down to soft drop, double-tap for hard drop.
